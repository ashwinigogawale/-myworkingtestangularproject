Frequntly asked question

Q1.Do you help with designing the outfit?

Yes. When you place a stitching order with us, we pair you up with one of our personal design consultants who will help design a look for your outfit. While you’re making this choice, she will also help you understand what kind of designs might suit your body type.

Q2.I have a design reference. Can you make me something that looks like it?

If you have inspiration from Pinterest, friends’ outfits, etc, we can use it as a starting point.

Q3.What are your charges?

You can check our pricing here

Q4.If my order has alterations, will you charge extra?

No, we do not. We try to get your measurements as accurately as possible, to avoid alterations. But in case your dress doesn’t fit, the cost of alteration is on us!

Q5.Can I add pockets to any type of clothing?

Yes, almost. We can add pockets to 90% of your clothes including dresses, kurtas, trousers, skirts etc. There are some cases where we cannot or it's not advisable. For example, we cannot add pockets to clothes that are very tight fitted or have no side seams.

Q6.When do I make the payment?

For stitching orders, you need to pay before delivery. However, you can rest assured that any alterations after delivery will be handled free of cost. If you’re procuring the fabric from us too, you need to pay for the fabric before we start making your garment.

Q7.How should I submit the measurements?

You just need to send us one perfectly-fitting garment along with your dress material. We will use that as a reference for measurements. Additionally, you can mention specifics that need to be customised to our design team..

Q8. Do you charge for pick-up and delivery?

Great news, pick-up and delivery service are free of charge!

Q9. Will my fabric and dress material be safe during this process?

You can pack the dress material and measurement garment in a secure way, before handing it over to our pick-up service. Else you can request for a packaging kit from us, which will be available at an additional cost. We will ensure your fabric and garment is taken care of, and handed back in good condition.

Q10.Is Service Tax Applicable?

Yes, service tax will be applied to your order in keeping with the Government rules and regulations.
